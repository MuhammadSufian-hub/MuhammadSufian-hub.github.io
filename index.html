77 GHz Automotive Radar Board

A custom board for the TI AWR2944 77 GHz radar chip. It runs 4 transmit and 4 receive channels for MIMO on a low loss Rogers laminate, brings power up in the right order in hardware, and talks CAN FD and Ethernet to the car.

Bare Metal STM32 DSP Receiver

Firmware written straight to the registers, with no HAL and no operating system. It samples at 2 million samples a second, works out the I and Q of a 29 kHz signal in real time, and streams the result to a PC over USB about 1000 times a second.
